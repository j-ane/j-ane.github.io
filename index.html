hey there, my name is jane zeng.
I take a design-thinking approach to everyday data science.

what do I mean by design-thinking in the context of data science? I believe a data scientist needs to balance being both data-driven and human-centered in order to creatively answer business problems. data science is all about diving into ambiguity, but developing a deep understanding of who we're designing for can shed light in the correct direction. I've come to learn that data science is more than just building models, it's about building models that are designed to be insightful and useful for the end user (whoever it may be).
